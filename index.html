HELLO, I am MARGARITA KHOVANSKAYA and everything about me and my life

ABOUT ME

Hello everyone, my name is Margarita Khovanskaya and I want to tell you a little about myself. I am 23 years old, I was born in the city of Baranovichi and now I also live in it. After graduating from school, university and receiving a master's degree, I work at the Social Protection Fund, but in my heart I have always loved and will love dancing. I started dancing as a child in majorette ensemble "HOLIDAY", where I continue to go to rehearsals and perform. This is a very famous and respected ensemble that has won prizes all over the world and I am very glad and proud that I have become a member and a part of this ensemble. On the way of my growing up with dancing, I never parted, and even when I entered the university and could not attend "Holiday" I still wanted to dance and continued to do it in the group "Sparkle dance" I created. The team even became successful performing and taking various prizes throughout Belarus. Dancing is my love which I will never part with.

The "Holiday" team is:

  • the winner of the International Festival of Brass Music "Belarusian Fanfares 2017" in the nomination "Best Majorette Group";
  • 13-time winner of international competitions among majorettes, cheerleaders and tambour majorettes;
  • a bronze medalist of the European Championship among majorettes and cheerleaders;
  • special guest of the National Day in France (2016);
  • participant of the semi-final of the folk show "Я МОГУ!" (RB);
  • Winner of the Grand Prix of the 29th International Festival of Brass Bands with Majorettes (Republic of Poland);
  • a participant in the opening and closing of International championships and tournaments in game kinds of sports, as well as the opening of Ice Palaces in Pruzhany, Bereza;
  • a participant in the event dedicated to the 70th anniversary of the Victory "Hero City - Minsk";
  • a participant in the opening of the World Rowing Championship (Brest);
  • a participant in the main events of Baranovichi, Minsk and Brest (City Holidays, Victory Day, Independence Day of the Republic of Belarus);
  • a participant in the opening of the Eurasian Games in Brest;
  • participant of regional and republican holidays "Dazhynki".
